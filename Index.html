02 — Studio

We design the quiet parts of a home first.

Most design decisions people notice — the sofa, the art, the light fixture — are the last ones we make. We start earlier: with how a room receives daylight through the day, where a family actually gathers, and which materials will still look considered after years of real use.

Founded in London, Halden Interiors works on a small number of residential projects at a time, from single rooms to full-home builds, for clients who'd rather wait for the right result than rush an average one.

Projects

01 The Chelsea Courtyard House Full-home, new build 2025

A five-bedroom family home built around a shaded central courtyard. Oak joinery and limewashed walls throughout, with brass detailing carried from the entry hall to the primary suite.

02 Belgravia Apartment Full renovation 2024

04 — How We Work

A four-stage process, start to move-in.

Stage One

Consultation

A walkthrough of your space and how you actually live in it — followed by a clear scope and timeline.

Stage Two

Concept & Materials

Layout options, material palette, and mood direction, presented for your sign-off before anything is ordered.

Stage Three

Sourcing & Build

We manage vendors, joinery, and site contractors directly, with weekly progress updates.

Stage Four

Styling & Handover

Final furnishing, styling, and a walkthrough — the home is ready to live in the day we hand over keys.
